Exclusive Experiences

Owning a Lamborghini means something. Something unique, special, exhilerating. It shows the same in you. This club aims to exemplify that beautiful lifestyle connection between class and craziness, by providing a means to showcase and enjoy your amazing Italian machines.

  • Dine and stay at some of UAEs most exclusive luxury hotels.
  • Mix with ladies and gentlemen who share your passion.
  • Drive the best roads in the UAE.
  • Join official events with Automobili Lamborghini around the world.

Membership is exclusive and faciliated one-on-one with our team. Your priviliges, committment and what to expect will be explained then.
